About Me

Mr. Ajit Sahoo is a highly respected legal and tax practitioner based in Angul, Odisha. He is known for his deep expertise in Customs, GST, Income Tax, and Sales Tax advisory, as well as tax optimization. With over a decade of experience in the field, he has built a solid reputation for providing exceptional tax and legal solutions that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

His proficiency spans a wide range of services including:

  • Customs Consultancy: Expert advice and assistance in customs duties, imports, and exports.
  • GST Tax Optimization: Tailored solutions for GST filing, planning, and optimization to reduce tax liabilities.
  • Income Tax Advisory: Personalized income tax planning, filing, and compliance services for individuals and businesses.
  • Sales Tax Solutions: Comprehensive services for sales tax compliance, GST, and VAT regulations.

Mr. Sahoo is dedicated to helping businesses and individuals navigate the complexities of these systems, ensuring compliance while optimizing tax liabilities to maximize financial benefits. His approach is focused on providing solution-oriented guidance to help clients achieve their financial and business objectives.
